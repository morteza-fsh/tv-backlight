This is Amdahl's Law:
  Speedup = 1 / ((1 - P) + P/S)
  Where P = 0.06 (6% is optimized), S = 4 (4x speedup)
  = 1 / ((1 - 0.06) + 0.06/4)
  = 1 / (0.94 + 0.015)
  = 1.047x (4.7% improvement)
